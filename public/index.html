Welcome to the triangle app

Test if the triangle is scalene, equilateral, isosceles, or neither of those. Choose between the three inputs represented by buttons "3 sides", "coordinates" and "angle" of the trianlge.

Triangle with three given sides

Insert the three sides of the triangle. By default, there is one joint point coordinates of sides A and C. There should be two intersection points of the circles (radiuses/triangle sides) to construct the triangle. E.g. try to insert 60 into side B, leaving other default coordinates.






Triangle with known sides coordinates

Insert your own triangle values for the points position x and y






Triangle with two given sides and the angle between them

Insert the two sides and the angle value. In order to construct the triangle cosine angle between side B and C will be calculated and the third side drawn.
